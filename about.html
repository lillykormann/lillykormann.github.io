About Me

I am a first year PhD student in Astrophysics at the University of Vienna, with a focus on the 3D structure of the local ISM. Currently, I am mainly working with Gaia-based 3D dust maps, which make it possible to trace the distribution of dust and gas in more detail than ever before!

Some of the 3D interactive figures related to my work can be found under the Research section of this website, I am always happy to chat about the visualization of 3D data and potential new tools.

On the side, I am an editor for the Star Formation Newsletter. If you are interested in star formation, would like to recieve the newsletter monthly, or want to submit a paper that fits the scope, feel free to visit us at starformationnews.org!

This website is still a work in progress, so I will update it as I go :)
